Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform.

1. What is this platform about?

Our platform helps users discover and purchase traditional state-wise products from different regions of India.

2. How can I place an order?

Browse products, add items to your cart, and complete checkout using secure online payment methods.

3. How can I become a seller?

Click on the “Become a Seller” option, fill out the registration form, upload required documents, and wait for admin approval.

Yes, sellers are verified before approval to ensure genuine and quality traditional products.

We support online payments including UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, and PayPal.

6. Can I cancel or return an order?

Yes, cancellation and return options are available according to our refund and return policy.

7. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through the Contact Us page or email us directly for assistance.
