About CultureSparsh

Connecting India’s rich cultural heritage with modern online shopping.

Who We Are

CultureSparsh is an online platform dedicated to showcasing traditional and handcrafted products from different states of India. Our mission is to preserve Indian culture, empower local artisans, and make authentic regional products accessible to everyone.

From Assam silk and Rajasthan handicrafts to Kashmir shawls and Kerala spices, we bring the beauty of India’s traditions directly to your doorstep.

Our Vision

We aim to create a trusted marketplace where skilled artisans, small businesses, and local sellers can grow their businesses while preserving the uniqueness of Indian craftsmanship.

“Every product tells a story of culture, tradition, and craftsmanship.”

Why Choose CultureSparsh?

Authentic Products

We promote genuine handmade and traditional items from verified sellers across India.

Support Local Artisans

Every purchase helps support artisans, craftsmen, and small local businesses.

Secure Shopping

Enjoy safe payments, trusted sellers, and a smooth online shopping experience.

Our Commitment

CultureSparsh is committed to promoting India’s rich heritage while embracing modern technology and e-commerce solutions. We believe in quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.
